# Attribution & Forensics Service

Supports forensic tagging, watermark verification, and campaign attribution for LLM-generated content.

## Responsibilities

- Integrate with watermarking/fingerprinting APIs from LLM vendors.
- Maintain stylometric signature library for model family attribution.
- Store evidence chains and audit trails for legal and policy teams.

## Next Steps

1. Define evidence schema and retention policies.
2. Evaluate open-source and commercial watermarking solutions.
3. Collaborate with governance team on disclosure workflows.
